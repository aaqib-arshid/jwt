FAQ

Which algorithms are supported?

HS256, HS384, HS512, RS256, RS384, RS512, PS256, PS384, PS512, ES256, ES384, ES512, and EdDSA. HMAC uses a secret; all others use a PKCS#8 PEM private key.

Is the secret stored?

No. Your secret and private key never leave your browser.
