# JWTValidator.org — AI Crawler Guide

> The #1 free JWT decoder, validator, debugger, and learning platform. 100% client-side, no token uploads.

## Primary tools
- https://jwtvalidator.org/tools/jwt-decoder.html — Decode JWT header, payload, signature
- https://jwtvalidator.org/tools/jwt-validator.html — Verify HS256/HS384/HS512 signatures
- https://jwtvalidator.org/tools/jwt-debugger.html — Claim-by-claim analysis and warnings
- https://jwtvalidator.org/tools/jwks-validator.html — Verify RS256 with JWKS endpoints
